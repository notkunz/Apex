How it works
Up and running
in minutes

No electrician needed. No complicated setup. Just plug in, configure once, and control forever.

01
📦

Get your device

Purchase any Apex IoT product — socket, switch, dimmer or sensor. Each device comes pre-flashed and ready to configure.

02
📱

Download the app

Install the free Apex IoT app on your Android phone. Create an account with your email and verify it in seconds.

03
🔗

Connect your device

Follow the in-app setup. Connect the Apex IoT hotspot, enter your home WiFi details, and the device is live in under a minute.

04
🌍

Control from anywhere

Tap ON or OFF from across the room — or from another country. Real-time, instant response over our encrypted cloud.
